## Diffie-Hellman Key Exchange explained
<details>
<summary><b>Reveal answer</b></summary>
1. Alice and Bob exchange their public key PAB<br>2. Alice computes F(SA,PAB); Bob computes F(SB,PAB)<br>3. The carefully chosen function F satisfies:<br>F(SA,PAB) = F(SB,PAB)<br><br>4. Alice and bob now share a secret
</details>
